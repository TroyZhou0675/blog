Here is a review of my recent class presentation.
On the positive side, my delivery was smooth, and I didn't have any significant issues with forgetting my lines or pausing. I was also able to make eye contact with the audience, and the content and format seemed to connect well with them.
However, there were clear areas for improvement. I tended to keep both hands on the microphone, which meant I used no body language. I also still felt stage fright, which likely contributed to my unnatural and aimless walking on stage.
