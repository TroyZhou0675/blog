**Beginning:**
"Christmas is just around the corner. When we mention this holiday, what immediately pops into your mind? For some of us, it’s a big, fancy meal. For others, it represents a romantic date with a boyfriend or girlfriend. But, did you know that the way we celebrate here is completely different from the rest of the world?"
"Let’s look at our neighbor, Japan.  Believe it or not, they have a very strange custom. They don't eat turkey; they eat **KFC**！

**End:**
"So, the next time you see a couple on a date or see an advertisement for a Christmas sale, remember this difference. The West celebrates 'Family,' while the East celebrates 'Fun.' Whether you are staying at home like a Westerner, eating KFC like a Japanese, or having a big meal with your crush here in China... there is no wrong way to celebrate. I hope you all have a wonderful time. Wish you merry Christmas in advance!"
