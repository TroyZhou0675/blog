Here is my preparation process: 
1. I created an outline for my speech based on the topic. 
2. I wrote the speech according to the outline. 
3. I revised the speech. 
4. I memorized the speech. 
5. I practiced in front of a mirror until I was proficient.
6. I did a practice run of the speech and timed it. I found that it was too long, so I shortened it appropriately. 
7. I continued to practice.
