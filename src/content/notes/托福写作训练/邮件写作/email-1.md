Dear Event Organizer:

  Due to an unexpected traffic accident on my way to campus, I had no choice but to leave my taxi and switch to a bus, which resulted in a significant delay.
  I sincerely regret missing the opening remarks and the introduction, as I understand they provided a valuable opportunity to learn about the event and meet fellow students.

  Thank you for your time and for organizing such a meaningful event!
  Sincerely,
  Troy
