Dear Library Committee: 
I'm writing to recommend a new software for our library management system. 
During exam weeks, many students will occupy the seats with their possessions but some of them do not arrive in time, which causes a waste of resources. And our current system does not have a service for students to check available seats and reserve them in a proper way.
Thus, I would like to recommend a time-limited reservation system Seats can be reserved on the software and will return available if a student doesn't arrive in time. This will prevent the phenomenon that some seats are occupied but no one sits there all day long. As a result, the library will run more smoothly and students will have more spaces to study without worrying about seats. 
Thank you for reading and I'd really appreciate it if you could take my recommendation into account.

Best regards 
Troy


Dear Library Committee:
I'm writing to recommend implementing a digital seat reservation system to improve our library management. During exam weeks, many students leave their belongings to occupy seats but not return **for long periods**. Unfortunately, our current system does not allow students to check seat availability or make proper reservation, which results in inefficient use of study place.
I suggest adopting a time-limited online reservation system that automatically cancels a booking if a student does not check in **within a certain period**. This system could also be integrated with our student ID database, making it more user-friendly and **compatible with** current library system.
By ensuring **fair seat allocation**, this software would significantly improve **operational efficiency** and enhance students' **overall** study experience. It would reduce conflicts over seating and create a more organized learning environment.
Thank you for considering my recommendation.
Best regards,
Troy
