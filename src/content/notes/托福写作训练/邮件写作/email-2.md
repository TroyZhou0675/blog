**Question:**
You are a university student who has noticed that the curtains in one of the frequently used classrooms have been damaged. This has caused difficulty in controlling sunlight during lectures and presentations, leading to discomfort for the students and the professors using the room.

Write an email to the campus maintenance office. In your email, do the following:
Describe the issue with the curtains in detail Mention how it is affecting the classroom environment Request a timely repair or replacement of the damaged curtains

**Answer:**
To: Campus Maintenance Office

Subject: Request for Curtain Repair in Classroom

Dear Campus Maintenance Office:
I'm writing to you to report that the curtain in Classroom 203 is damaged.
The curtain is stuck due to the rust on its track, and  it cannot be fully opened or closed. As a result, sunlight shines directly on the whiteboard in the afternoon, making it difficult for students on the right side of the classroom to see the board clearly due to the glare. This has disrupted lectures and affected the overall environment.
I would appreciate it if you could arrange a repair or replacement at your earliest convenience.
Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Best regards 

Troy
