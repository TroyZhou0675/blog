Dear Library Manager: 
I'm writing to report an issue that there is a shortage of electrical outlets in the library, causing inconvenience for students. 
As the teaching approach in our university becomes increasingly digital, students need their laptops nearly all the time. What's more, students majoring in engineering or science often use high-performance laptops to run simulations, which consume battery power quickly. For this reason, students who cannot find an outlet may be unable to finish their homework on time.
To solve this problem, more electrical outlets should be installed in places like the bottom of the desks during the upcoming renovation. In addition, charging stations can be added as a temporal solution.
I would appreciate it if you could take my recommendation into account.

Best regards,
Troy.

electric是用电驱动的，比如：electric guitar , electric car
electrical是和电有关的，比如：electrical engineering 
homework: uncountable noun
upcoming renovation:后续的翻新
major in engineering
