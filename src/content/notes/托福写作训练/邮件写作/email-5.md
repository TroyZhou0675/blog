You recently ordered a product from an online store and received it last week. However, you encountered issues with the delivery process and the condition of the item when it arrived. You would like to provide feedback to the store so that they can improve their service in the future.  
  
Write an email to the customer service department of the online store. In your email, do the following:  
- Explain the details of your order.  
- Describe the issues you faced with the delivery.  
- Suggest possible improvements to prevent similar issues in the future.

Dear Customer Service Department: 
I'm writing to give my feedback about issues with the delivery process and the condition of the item.
Last Monday, I bought a marvel comic book from your online store and chose truck delivery which was promised to arrive in 2 days. However, when I checked the delivery details on website last Thursday, the item was still on the way. Moreover, when I finally received my item last Friday, I found a corner of the book was visibly creased. 
I believe several improvements would be helpful to avoid similar issues. To make delivery more punctual, according punishment can be adopted. Further more, better protective package can be applied to secure the integrity of the item. 
I would appreciate it if you could take my recommendation into account.

Best regards Troy

修改后文章：
Dear Customer Service Department
I am writing to provide feedback regarding a recent order I places with your store. Last Monday, I purchased a Marvel comic book and selected standard delivery, which was expected to arrive within two days.
However, the package did not arrive on time. When I checked the tracking information on your website last Thursday, it was still in transit. The item was finally delivered on Friday, which was later than promised. Furthermore, when I opened the package, I noticed that one corner of the book had been visibly creased, likely due to insufficient protective packaging.
To prevent similar issues in the future, I would suggest improving coordination with shipping partners to ensure timely delivery. In addition, using stronger packaging materials, such as bubble warp or reinforced cardboard, could help protect delicate items during transit.
Thank you for your attention. I hope my suggestions will be helpful in improving your service.
