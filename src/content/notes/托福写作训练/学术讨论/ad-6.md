I believe that emotional intelligence is more importance than technical skills in the workplace because it improves working efficiency and overall productivity.
First, large projects always requires many individuals to work together thus good communication skills are essential. People with high emotional intelligence are better at understanding others feelings and manage conflicts, which allows teams to collaborate more effectively are complete tasks more efficiently.
In addition, emotionally intelligent employees can build strong connections with their coworkers. This not only strengthens relationships among teams members but also increases team cohesion and creates a more supportive working environment.
Admittedly, some people may argue that technical skills are more important because many projects rely on specific knowledge. However, technical shortcomings can often be improved through experience and teamwork, while poor communication skills are harder to develop quickly.
