**Structure1:**

Point&Elaboration + Further explanation + Example + Concession

_**I believe** grading shall focus on students’ performance on assignments and tests, since it’s the most effective way to measure the progress in study._

_**To be more specific,** performance on assignments and tests best reflect students’ understanding of academic concepts and their problem-solving skills, which are also the objective of most courses._

_**For example,** the score on a mathematics test indicates if the student have completely understood formulas and theories, and if they can apply them in various conditions._

_**Still,** some may claim that student learn something beneficial to their life through hard effort regardless of performance. **However,** we all need to learn from our mistakes and correct them in time, instead of persisting on wrong ideas or approaches._
