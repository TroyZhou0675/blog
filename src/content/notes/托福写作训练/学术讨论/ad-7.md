I **contend** that broad social and economic forces **exert** a greater influence **on the course of history**, as they **constitute** the **intrinsic power** that drive the advancement of civilization.
Specifically, the people are the **architects** of society, producing the material conditions **essential for** its functioning through their labor. They also serve ad the engines of social revolutions, facilitating the transformation and progress of social formations. Economic forces, meanwhile, act as the decisive determinants in the evolution enhancing productivity, set in motion the transition from feudalism to capitalism.
Critics may argue that **pivotal historical events** are often shaped by **prominent** individuals. While the **initiatives** of certain leaders or innovators can have immediate and visible impacts, these effects are ultimately constrained by the broader social and economic context. **In essence**, historical figures may influence the trajectory of events, but they do not create the fundamental material conditions that underpin the development of society and civilization.

- contend for 争夺
