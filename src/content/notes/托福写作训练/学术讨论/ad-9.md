  I believe it is a good idea for the government to impose stricter penalties on companies that cause environmental damage
---

I believe it is a good idea for the government to impose stricter penalties on companies that cause environmental damage, since this policy can not only reduce pollution but also promote eco-friendly technology.
To elaborate, with more taxes and fines imposed on polluting companies, production becomes more costly, which in turn discourages excessive production and ultimately leads to lower levels of pollution.
In addition, these penalties will force companies to adopt cleaner production methods, which can promote the development of green technology. For example, under air pollution taxes, companies in chemical industry tend to develop new technologies to reduce harmful gas emissions.
