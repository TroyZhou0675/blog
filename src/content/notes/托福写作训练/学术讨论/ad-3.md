I believe high school students should not be forced to do volunteer work, since it goes against the original intention of volunteer activities and reduces students' enthusiasm.
To elaborate, mandatory volunteer programs usually limit **participation** to a narrow range of activities, meaning that other forms of volunteer work cannot **be counted toward** the required service hours. This pattern significantly **discourages students from** engaging in volunteer work. In addition, mandatory volunteer work may reduce overall quality of volunteer activities. Some students may participate only because they have to, which means they may not devote enough effort or responsibility to the work.
Some people argue that mandatory volunteer programs can encourage students to participate in community service. **That said,** forcing students to volunteer may actually **undermine** the true spirit of volunteerism.
